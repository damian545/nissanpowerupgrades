Power Upgrades:

When you are looking for more power, your cooling system should be upgraded at the same time because of the heat your car will generate getting this power. Don't blow up your engine and waste your money in doing so either..

Turbo:

The turbo should be a reliable unit ie efficient at compressing air and roller bearing. There are a lot of options out there and some are not worth the money because the quality of manufacturing isn't there. Buy a reliable name, like Garrett Turbos. They have been around since 1950 and they know there product, they use to make HKS trubos back in the day. When they stopped making them, they made a new line of turbos called GTX. They are a billet wheeled turbo with 11 blades on the inlet of the turbo, in Adelaide there is a bussiness called MTQES. They stock GTX turbos and can offer you a waste gate to be preset at any psi you wish for peace of mind, but yor stock one should fit..
