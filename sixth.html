The Grip and Control:

Drift handling or Street handling:



The final important part to build your car is grip, even in drifting you need front end grip. So good off-set wheels are required, If you can not find any, get as wide as you can and add bolt on spacers. I had +38 fronts and +45 rears on my skyline, as was still street driven at the time. So I added 10 mm bolt on front spacers and 20 mm rear bolt on spacers, it gave me the look and some grip too. But at the end of the day, the width is still needed. 8 inch fronts and 9 inch rears were not enough, so I got 9 inch fronts with +20 and 10 inch rears with +18. So much better at high speed drifting.. 3 piece wheels offer a great skope for getting off-set, by changing the inner or outer drum of the wheel to suit to look or for the grip. Stretched tyres are a must for all occasions in my eyes, perfect look for most cars..
